This is a simple application designed to validate "word ladder" puzzles.

A word ladder is a type of word puzzle made up of a series of words, called 'rungs'. Each word varies from the immediately preceeding word by one, and ony one, letter. For example feed → feel. The words in the ladder can be almost any length (minimum length of 3 letters) but they must all be the same length. There may be any number of words in the ladder.

This page checks for word length, duplicate words, missing rungs (more than one letter changed in a single word pair) and 'bungs'. Bungs are word triplets where the same letter is changed; for example feed → feel → feet. Bungs are allowed, but unnecessary, and are considered bad form.
